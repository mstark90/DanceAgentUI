About

Lately it seems that I've been in high demand for swing dances at Pla-Mor and at Bushwackers, so I'm going to have to take drastic measures to ensure people get a dance in. This means that I'm going to institute a reservation system because I can only dance so much in one night.

As much as I would want to fulfill every request when asked, I can't remember them all and sometimes I get pulled on the dance floor trying to fulfill the requests. This way, I feel I can make sure a bit better that everyone who wants a dance can get a dance.

Also, I wanted to make an app and I felt this was a good way to do it.
